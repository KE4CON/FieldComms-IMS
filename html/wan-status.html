Antenna Quick-Reference

ANTENNATYPEWHEN TO USESETUP TIME
InstyConnect Drum Omnidirectional 5G/LTE Default — deploy at every activation. No aiming needed. ~2 min
InstyConnect Switchblade Directional 4×LDAP folding When Drum signal is poor. Swap PoE cable to same ASUS WAN port. Aim toward nearest tower using InstyConnect signal app. ~5 min
Starlink dish Satellite (automatic failover) Automatic — ASUS switches to Starlink USB WAN when cellular drops. Requires clear sky view. Auto
Switchblade swap procedure: Unplug Drum PoE cable from ASUS WAN port → Plug Switchblade PoE cable into same port → Open InstyConnect Wi-Fi on a phone → Browse to http://my.insty → Rotate Switchblade slowly while watching signal bars → Lock antenna direction at peak signal.
